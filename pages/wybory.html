#wyboryparlamentarne2019

This poster was created for the 2019 sejm and senate elections in Poland. Elections in Poland hit particularly hard, as we have a strongly conservative government currently ruling. In the past years, less than half of the population of Poland would vote. This poster was meant to join into the campaign rallying young people and others to come out and vote in these particular elections.
